Our Bouldering Trip!

Boulder #1

One of the best of the grade the Holies has to offer. Starting on a low jug, move up to a slopey sidepull, do some magic with your feet and reach your left hand up to a bad crimp. Get your feet up, and do a tricky move to a blocky jug in the middle of the face.

Boulder #2

Named for the seafood green colored lichen on the boulder, this excellent sloping, vertical Crete doesn't receive the love and attention it deserves. Climb arete on the right side only. A few perfect holds, couple good feet, and a committing move to a jug finish. Grad a couple pads and a trusted scooter, and you'll enjoy this unsung gem.

Boulder #3

These boulders are located just downhill from Shadow of a Man. Find a trail just last Seams Easy that leads downhill about 150 feet. This problem climbs the right arete on the left boulder.

Holy Boulders

The Holy Boulders is the largest concentration of boulders in Southern Illinois. There are over 200 developed boulder problems from V0 to V13 and has the potential for hundreds more. The sandstone boulders and cliff line are known for their Fontainebleau-like perfect friction and aesthetic lines.
